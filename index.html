AI-Assisted Parts Management

Receive, log, and find every part by talking to the Genius and snapping a photo — then check your stock from anywhere.

Receive in seconds

Snap it, say it, done.

  • Photograph the data plate and 8130-3 — the Genius reads the part number, serial, and trace
  • Tell it out loud where it's going, what it cost, and the condition
  • The full receiving record builds itself while your hands stay on the part

Point and identify

Aim your camera, get the answer.

  • Point at a part on the shelf and pull up its record, condition, and paperwork
  • Verify you're holding the right serial before it goes on the airplane
  • No digging through bins to figure out what you've got

One living parts database

Every part, every detail, in one place.

  • Part number, serial, condition, cost, and bin location — all captured on intake
  • 8130-3 and traceability stored with the part it belongs to
  • Nothing lives on a sticky note or in someone's head

Check stock from anywhere

Your stockroom, in your pocket.

  • Look up what's on the shelf from the road, the ramp, or another base
  • Know whether you've already got the part before you buy or drive back
  • Real-time — what you see is what's actually on hand

* AI-Assisted Parts Management is available as an add-on module and may require additional configuration to maintain.

Remote Viewing

Eyes on, in real time

  • Stream live from the tech's or pilot's camera, hands-free

  • Talk it through together instead of describing it over a bad phone line

Whoever you need on the glass

The right person, not just anyone available.

  • One tap from the field, no scheduling a callback

Full context, not starting cold

  • Parts already on hand show up in the same view
